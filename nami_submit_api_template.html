How to Use Nami Wallet and SPO Direct Node Link

STEP 1 of 6

Open your Nami Wallet browser Extention

If you have not setup a Nami wallet yet you need to either create a new wallet or import your wallet to Nami. Visit https://namiwallet.io/

STEP 2 of 6

Once you open your Nami Wallet in your browser you should see your wallet details.

Click on your profile image in the top right.

STEP 3 of 6

Once you click on your profile your Account menu will pop-up.

Then click "Setting".

STEP 4 of 6

Once in Settings, Click on the “Network”.

STEP 5 of 6

Make sure slider is on Mainnet.

Click "Custom Node" Check box.

STEP 6 of 6

Now paste your prefer SPO link found on freeloaderz.io into the text box and click "Apply".

CONGRATULATIONS YOU ARE NOW SET FOR FASTER TRANSACTIONS DUE TO DIRECT SPO NODE LINK
